# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 15, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how StreamHub ("the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive the information you authorize Apple to share.

### Usage Data
We may collect basic usage data such as app session activity to support features like the daily watch timer.

### User-Provided Content
Stream sources and favorites you add are stored to provide the App's functionality.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the App's functionality
- Manage your account and subscription status
- Track daily watch time for free-tier users
- Store your favorites and stream sources
- Improve the App experience

## 3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud-hosted database service. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:
- **Apple StoreKit** — for subscription management and payment processing
- **Sign in with Apple** — for authentication
- **Supabase** — for backend data storage and authentication

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing the use of your information.

## 5. Subscriptions and Payments

All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card numbers, or financial data.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties, except as required to operate the App (as described in Third-Party Services above) or as required by law.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your associated data will be removed from our systems.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Your Rights

You may:
- Access your account data through the App
- Delete your account and associated data through the App's settings
- Contact us to request information about your data

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at fabiangatter@icloud.com.
